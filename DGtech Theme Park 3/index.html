About Us

DGtech Theme Park is a futuristic, tech-oriented park offering a variety of rides, exhibitions, and interactive experiences. Located in the heart of the city, we provide an unforgettable experience for visitors of all ages. Our operating hours are from 10 AM to 10 PM daily. Tickets can be purchased online or at the entrance.
